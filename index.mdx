## What CrestPet does

A unified platform for every aspect of veterinary care.

  <Card title="Appointments and Scheduling" icon="calendar-check" iconType="duotone" color="#06b6d4">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Electronic Health Records" icon="clipboard-list" iconType="duotone" color="#06b6d4">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Pet Wellness" icon="heart-pulse" iconType="duotone" color="#06b6d4">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments and Billing" icon="credit-card" iconType="duotone" color="#06b6d4">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise Analytics" icon="chart-line" iconType="duotone" color="#06b6d4">
