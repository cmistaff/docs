## What CrestPet does

A unified platform for every aspect of veterinary care.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Appointments and Scheduling" icon="calendar-check">
    Real-time availability, slot reservations, video consultations, and referral booking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Electronic Health Records" icon="clipboard-list">
    Consultation notes, dental charts, diagnostic requests, imaging, treatment and anesthetic sheets.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pet Wellness" icon="heart-pulse">
    AI-powered health scoring, wellness plans, and video summaries delivered to pet owners.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments and Billing" icon="credit-card">
    Stripe Connect, invoicing, insurance splits, payouts, and staff commissions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Inventory and Purchasing" icon="boxes-stacked">
    Stock tracking, lot management, low-stock alerts, auto-reorder, and PO workflows.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    Multi-location dashboards, predictive analytics, and centralized clinic oversight.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Marcia AI Assistant" icon="robot">
    Chat, voice, SMS, and WhatsApp channels for clients and staff -- powered by Marcia.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance and Documents" icon="file-shield">
    DocuSign templates, consent forms, legal policies, and PDF certificate generation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The Eight Portals

CrestPet is organized into eight portals, each accessed through a dedicated subdomain.

<CardGroup cols={4}>
  <Card title="Clinic" icon="stethoscope">
    Primary workspace for veterinary practices. Appointments, EHR, billing, inventory, whiteboards, and staff ops.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Consumer" icon="paw">
    Pet owner portal for booking, health records, medications, vaccinations, and clinic messaging.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise" icon="building">
    Multi-location management for corporate vet groups -- analytics, staff, subscriptions, oversight.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Platform" icon="shield-halved">
    Admin control panel. Tenants, users, species data, feature policies, AI settings, revenue.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referral" icon="share-nodes">
    Inter-clinic patient referrals with availability-based scheduling and full audit logging.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Marcia" icon="robot">
    AI veterinary assistant -- chat, voice (ElevenLabs), SMS, WhatsApp, training data, draft generation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vendor" icon="truck">
    Frontend portal for vendor purchase order confirmation pages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="API" icon="code">
    Public REST API surface -- OpenAPI spec, authenticated tenant access, rate limiting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Tech stack

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Frontend">
    **Next.js 15** with App Router, **React 19**, **TypeScript 5.8**, **Tailwind CSS 4**. Mobile clients via **Expo (React Native)**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Backend">
    **NestJS 11** on **Node.js 22**, **TypeScript 5.8**, **Prisma 6** ORM. **pnpm 10** workspaces.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Database">
    **PostgreSQL** on **Amazon RDS** (Aurora-compatible). Multi-tenant schema with row-level isolation.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Cache">
    **Redis** on **ElastiCache** -- session storage, rate limiting, queue backplane, real-time fan-out.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Auth">
    **Better Auth** with **JWT** sessions, **Prisma adapter**, and **Passkey/WebAuthn** support across portals.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Runtime">
    **Node.js 22**, **pnpm 10**, **Bun test runner**. CI on self-hosted GitHub Actions runners.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Infrastructure">
    **AWS ECS Fargate**, **EKS multi-region**, **RDS**, **ElastiCache**, **S3**, **CloudFront**, **ALB**, **WAF**, **Cloudflare** edge.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API and Testing">
    **Orval** auto-generates clients from the **OpenAPI** spec. **Bun test** for server unit + E2E. **Playwright** for frontend E2E.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
