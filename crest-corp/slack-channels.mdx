This page lists every active public Slack channel in the Crest workspace, grouped by prefix. Snapshot pulled from the Slack API on 2026-04-19. There are 37 active public channels (private channels and DMs are not listed here).

Crest follows a loose prefix convention. The buckets below reflect what is actually in the workspace today, not an aspirational naming policy.

- `crest-*` covers cross-team workspaces, alerts, and per-lead team rooms.
- `crestpet-*` covers the main product (CrestPetSystem) day-to-day.
- `dev-*` covers dev squads working a specific slice (bugs, features, UI, security).
- `daily-*` covers recurring async standups and report drops.
- `project-*` covers a single product or initiative.
- `p-*` is reserved for automated project alert routing (Sentry, bugs).
- `*-tips` is for tooling-specific Q&A (Cursor, Claude Code, Codex, Marcia CLI).

If the channel you need is not here, it is probably private. Ask your manager or post in `#all-crest`.

## Where to Ask for Help

The five most useful channels for a new hire in their first week.

| Channel | Members | When to use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `#all-crest` | 30 | Company-wide announcements and any general question that does not have an obvious home. Read-mostly, but you can post here if you do not know where else to go. |
| `#project-crestpetsystem` | 34 | Product questions about CrestPetSystem: features, behavior, "how does X work," PRs that need a second pair of eyes. Highest-traffic product channel. |
| `#crestpet-onboarding` | 28 | Setup, environment, devbox, and "I cannot get the local stack running" questions. Look at pinned tips before posting. |
| `#daily-updates` | 32 | Post your daily standup here using the format in the channel topic. Read other people's posts to see what the team is working on. |
| `#bug-reports` | 34 | File bugs you find in the product or tooling. Tag `@channel` only if it is blocking production. |

For private 1-to-1 questions (access, HR, payroll, paid time off, anything sensitive) DM your manager or Michael directly.
