<Warning>
**Non-negotiable.** Every developer and staff member must complete their Deel.com profile and pass a background check before getting access to Crest systems. If you do not complete the background check, you will not be able to work on this project, or any other Crest project, moving forward with us. This is non-negotiable.
</Warning>

This page mirrors the "Before You Start" segment of the onboarding video. Watch the video first if you have not already (see [Welcome](/crest-corp/welcome)), then come back here for the practical steps.

## Why we require it

Crest engineers touch live veterinary practice data, payment systems, AI infrastructure, and customer accounts. We have to know who is in our environments. The background check is one of the contractual gates that lets us tell our customers, our insurers, and our regulators that everyone with access has been vetted.

A failed or skipped background check is a contract gating event. It is not an "I will get to it later" item.

## What is required

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete your Deel.com profile">
    Government-issued ID, address proof, tax form for your country, and banking details for payouts. Step-by-step instructions are on the [Deel Profile Setup](/crest-corp/deel-setup) page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the background check inside Deel">
    Deel runs the background check through their own vetted vendor. The authorization step is part of your Deel onboarding flow. You will receive a separate email from the background check provider with any additional information they need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Respond promptly to vendor requests">
    The vendor may ask for a clarification, a second document, or proof of address. Respond the same business day. Stalled responses are the most common reason a background check takes more than a week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for confirmation from Bryant or Karen">
    Once the check clears, Bryant or Karen will notify you and provisioning of dev box, Tailscale, GitHub, AWS, and other access begins. Do not ask for early access; we cannot grant it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Timeline

Typical end-to-end time is **3 to 7 business days**, assuming you respond to vendor requests on the same business day. International checks (multiple country residences in the last 7 years, name changes, multi-language documents) can take longer. If your situation is unusual, flag it to Bryant on day one rather than week two.

## What happens during the wait

While the background check is running you can:

- Watch the onboarding video (`v6` linked from [Welcome](/crest-corp/welcome))
- Read the [Engineering Rules](/onboarding/engineering-rules)
- Read the [Engineering Onboarding Handbook](/onboarding/engineering-handbook)
- Read the [Code of Conduct](/crest-corp/code-of-conduct)

What you cannot do:

- Get a dev box
- Get Tailscale, GitHub, AWS, Slack, Linear, or Cursor access
- Attend standups other than the introductory one
- See or touch any source code

## If the check does not clear

If the background check returns adverse results, Bryant or Karen will contact you directly to discuss next steps. The contract may be paused or terminated depending on findings. Crest follows the consumer-reporting requirements of your jurisdiction; you have the right to dispute findings with the vendor.

## Who to contact

- **Status questions:** Bryant or Karen
- **Vendor portal issues:** the email contact listed in your background-check vendor invitation
- **Anything else:** DM Michael in Slack
