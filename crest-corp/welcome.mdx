Welcome to Crest. We are the team behind **MarciaOS**, **Marcia Code**, **Ask Marcia**, and **Crest Pet System**. This tab is the audience-first home for company-level policies that apply to anyone doing work with Crest, regardless of which product or repo you are touching.

<Note>
Before you write a single line of code, work your way through every page in **Required Setup**. The order matters. Skipping steps (especially SentinelOne EDR before Tailscale) will cost you a day of access provisioning.
</Note>
