## Overview

This document outlines how **Stripe payments and payouts** are implemented in the appointment booking scheduler system.
It covers the modules involved, appointment and payment flows, invoicing, fees, and payout distribution.
The goal is to provide a **clear reference for both technical and non-technical team members**.

---

## Major Modules

There are four main modules in the system:

1. **Clinic**
2. **Consumer**
3. **Enterprise**
4. **Super Admin**


User can be a part of multiple **consumer portals** (e.g., *Paweasy*, *Vetcorp*).

---

## Consumer Portals & Pet Profiles

- Within a consumer portal, a user sets up **pet profiles** (via documents or manual entries).
- A user can share pet profiles with clinics.
- Clinics can then create **appointments** against their services.


---

## Appointments & Steps

- Each appointment can consist of **multiple appointment steps** (e.g., X-rays, CT-scans).
- Each appointment step is **linked to a clinic service**.


---

## Service Payment Methods

Clinics can define payment methods for services:

- **Full Upfront** → Full service amount is shown and paid.
- **Deposit** → Only the initial deposit is shown and paid upfront. Remaining deposit is paid later.
- **Escrow** → Full service amount is shown, but payout is held in escrow until completion.


---

## Appointment Payment Flow

1. When an appointment is booked, the **consumer portal** shows the appointment as pending payment.
2. The consumer clicks **“Pay Now”**, opening the **payment page**.
3. The **bill** includes:Service amount(s)
4. Stripe fees
5. For multiple steps, amounts are aggregated along with **dynamically calculated Stripe fees**.
6. After successful payment:A **payment transaction** is created against the appointment.
7. A **payout transaction** is created against the payment transaction.
8. An **invoice entry** is generated in the DB.
9. **Invoice line items** are created for:Each appointment step
10. Stripe fees


---

## Due Dates & Late Fees

- Clinics can configure **due dates** and **late fees** in their settings.
- **Due date**: Any date between the current date and the appointment step date.
- **Late fee**: Can be a fixed amount or a percentage.
- If the due date is reached:The payment screen adds the late fee.
- A new **invoice line item** is created for the late fee.


---

## Payout Distribution

- Stripe deducts their processing fees.
- **5% platform fee** is retained in the main account.
- Remaining payout goes to the clinic’s **connected Stripe account**.


---

## Payment Method Scenarios

### Full Upfront

- Consumer pays the complete amount.
- Funds are distributed immediately (minus Stripe + platform fees).

### Deposit

- Initial deposit is paid.
- Appointment step is marked as **partially paid**.
- When the clinic/doctor marks the appointment step as complete, the **final deposit** is automatically charged.

### Escrow

- Full amount is paid upfront.
- Funds are held in escrow.
- When the appointment step is marked as complete, or when the step’s end time is reached, the **escrow releases**.
- Clinic receives payout (minus Stripe + platform fees).


---

## Currency Handling

- Clinics select their **preferred currency** in settings.
- A custom **currency conversion API** ensures:When creating a service step, the selected currency is converted into **USD**.
- On the frontend, prices are shown in the clinic’s selected currency.

**USD is the base currency** stored in the database.
---

## Post-Payment Appointment Updates

- After an appointment is created and paid for:Doctors can add **new steps** to the existing appointment.
- In the consumer portal, the appointment will again display a **“Pay Now”** button for unpaid steps.
- On payment:The new step’s line item is added to the existing invoice.
- The clinic’s **transactions screen** updates with the new step and overall payments.


---

## Transactions & Visibility

- **Clinic module**: Clinics see all their payment and payout transactions.
- **Consumer module**: Consumers see pending and completed payments for appointments.
- **Super Admin (platform)**: Admin can view all transactions, including the 5% platform fee.
- Clinics cannot create services or networks until **Stripe onboarding is complete**.


---

## Summary

- Every appointment results in **one invoice**.
- Each appointment step + Stripe fee generates **invoice line items**.
- Payments follow different flows depending on **Full Upfront, Deposit, or Escrow**.
- Stripe automatically handles deductions, while the platform fee (5%) is retained.
- Late fees, currency conversions, and post-payment updates are fully supported.
- All stakeholders (consumer, clinic, super admin) have visibility into their transactions.
