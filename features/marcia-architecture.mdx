# Marcia AI - Complete Architecture Documentation

**Author:** hamza
**Source:** Personal Confluence space at cammaratainc.atlassian.net

**Marcia** is a multi-channel AI-powered veterinary assistant embedded throughout the CrestPetSystem platform. It provides intelligent conversational AI capabilities across 4 communication channels (Web Portal, SMS, WhatsApp, Voice), leveraging 70+ MCP tools and 42+ specialised subagents to help pet owners and veterinary clinics with:

- Pet health management and assessments
- Appointment booking and clinic discovery
- Document analysis (health records, insurance, lab results)
- AI-powered scans (poop analysis, tooth scans)
- Product recommendations and shopping
- Treatment plan tracking
- Network subscription management
