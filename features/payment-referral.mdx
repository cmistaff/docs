## Overview

This document explains the **Stripe payment integration and flow** for appointments created through the **Referral System**.
There are **two types of referrals**:

**Third-Party Clinic → Crest Clinic**

**Clinic-to-Clinic (both under Crest)**

---

## 1. Third-Party Clinic → Crest Clinic

### Referral Portal & Onboarding

- Third-party clinics register on the **Referral Portal**.
- On first login, they see a **banner/tile** prompting them to connect their **Stripe account**.
- Referrals cannot be made until **Stripe onboarding** is completed.
- Once onboarding is complete, the clinic is redirected back to the **dashboard** and can now make referrals.

---

### Referral Creation

The referrer fills out a **Referral Form** containing:

- Pet Owner Information
- Pet Information
- Service Name

#### Service Selection

- Based on the service, the system shows **clinics offering that service**.
- The referrer selects a clinic → then selects an **available time slot**.
- The referrer can also view the **commission percentage** they will earn for that service.

---

### Backend Processing

System checks if the **pet owner already exists** in the platform.

- If not, a **new user** is created based on the referral information.

The **pet owner receives an email** with a button.

- Clicking it redirects them to the **Consumer Portal**.
- They set a password and verify their phone number.
- After verification, they are logged in.

On login, the **pet owner is already part of the Consumer Portal tenant**.

- Their **pet profile** is automatically created using the referral form details.

---

### Clinic Side

- In the **Referral Module**, the clinic sees the referral.
- The clinic can **Accept** or **Reject** the referral:Reject → The flow ends.
- Accept → An **appointment is created** for the referred pet owner at the chosen slot.The appointment is immediately in **Confirmed** state.

The pet owner receives an **SMS confirmation**.

The appointment also appears in the **Consumer Portal Appointment Module**.

---

### Payment Flow

- The pet owner must pay for the appointment using **Stripe**.
- On Stripe:**Consumer is charged:** Service Fee + Stripe Fee.
- **Stripe deducts its processing fee.**
- From the **Service Fee**:Referral Commission is deducted.
- Platform Fee is deducted.
- Remaining amount goes to the **Crest Clinic**.

#### Example Calculation

- Service Fee = 100%
- Commission = 25%
- Platform Fee = 5% (deducted from clinic’s share)
- Clinic receives = 70%

---

## 2. Clinic-to-Clinic Referral (Both Under Crest)

### Referral Creation

- From the **Appointment Module**, a clinic can refer a patient directly to another Crest clinic.
- The referral form and flow are the same as the **Third-Party Clinic referral system**.

---

### Pet Owner & Pet Profile

When the referral is created:

The **pet owner** and **pet profile** are automatically added to the **target clinic tenant** and its **Consumer Portal**.

---

### Remaining Flow

The rest of the flow (clinic acceptance, appointment creation, payment processing, and fund distribution) is the **same as Third-Party referrals**.

---

## Summary

**Third-party referrals** require Stripe onboarding before referrals can be created.

**Referrals generate appointments** automatically when accepted by the target clinic.

**Pet owners are onboarded automatically**, including email verification, password setup, and pet profile creation.

**Stripe handles payments** with clear fund distribution:

Commission + Platform Fee deducted.

Remaining amount paid to the Crest clinic.

**Clinic-to-clinic referrals** follow the same process, except both clinics are already under Crest.
