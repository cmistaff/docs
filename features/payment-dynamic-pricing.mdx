## Overview

This document explains how **Stripe payments and subscriptions** are integrated with the **Networking and Dynamic Pricing System**.
It covers the process of creating networks and plans, subscription handling, payouts, and the current limitations of the implementation.

---

## Modules Involved

- **Clinic**
- **Consumer**
- **Enterprise**
- **Super Admin**


---

## Networking & Plans

- Each **enterprise or clinic** can create their own **networks**.
- Within a network, network owners (clinic/enterprise) can **create subscription plans**.
- Network owners (clinic/enterprise) can invite other clinics into their network.
- Invited clinics can add their own **perks** to the plan (e.g., discounts, benefits).


---

## Stripe Onboarding Requirement

- Clinics cannot create networks until their **Stripe onboarding** is complete.


---

## Plan Creation & Stripe Integration

- When a clinic creates a plan in the **frontend network module**:A corresponding plan is created on **Stripe (main account, not connected accounts)**.
- Stripe returns a **Plan ID** and **Price ID**.
- These IDs are saved in the **connected database** along with the plan’s information.


---

## Subscription Flow for Consumers

1. Consumers view available plans in the **Network Tab** or **Subscription Module**.
2. When subscribing:Consumers enter **card details**.
3. They are subscribed to the plan according to its **billing cycle** (monthly or yearly).
4. Consumers can cancel their subscription:Cancelation does **not** immediately stop the subscription.
5. Instead, the subscription continues until the end of the already-paid cycle.


---

## Payment Flow

- When a consumer pays for a plan:The payment amount is **plan price + Stripe fees**.
- Funds are deposited into the **main account**.
- **95%** of the amount is sent to the **network creator**.
- **5% platform fee** remains in the main account.


---

## Discount Handling

- **Dynamic Discount Handling** is now implemented on the **backend**.
- Consumers can see the discount:When booking the appointment and on the booked appointment page as well.
- On the payment checkout page
- In invoice line items

The **clinic’s transaction page** now also shows how much discount was applied for tracking.

## Current Completion Status

✅ Fully implemented at **Clinic level** and **Consumer level**.
⚠️ Partial or missing in other areas.

---

## Current Limitations & Missing Features

1. **Enterprise Level**Frontend networking module is complete.
2. Stripe integration is **not yet implemented**.
3. **Database & Admin Visibility**No **payment or payout transaction entries** are recorded in the DB.
4. As a result:Nothing appears in **Super Admin dashboards**.
5. No **invoice** or **invoice line items** are generated for consumers.
6. **Currency Handling**No **dynamic currency conversion** implemented during checkout.
7. Payments are processed in a single fixed currency only.
8. **Dispute Management**No dispute handling implemented for:Appointment booking payments.
9. Subscription payments.


---

## Summary

- Networking system allows clinics/enterprises to create **plans**, invite other clinics, and add perks.
- Consumers can **subscribe and cancel** subscriptions, with payments flowing through Stripe.
- Stripe integration currently ensures plan creation and subscription but is **incomplete at enterprise and admin levels**.
- Several key features (currency handling, invoices, dispute management) remain to be implemented.
