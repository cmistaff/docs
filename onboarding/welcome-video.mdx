Welcome to Crest. This page is the canonical entry point for new engineers (and a refresher for existing staff). It hosts two artifacts: a short walkthrough video and a printable PDF reference. Both cover the same ground as the full [Engineering Onboarding Handbook](/onboarding/engineering-handbook), distilled for fast consumption on day one.

<Note>
  If you are pre-boarding (signed contract, not yet first-day), download the PDF below. Your Deel profile folder also contains a copy. The video is the day-one watch-along.
</Note>

<Note>
  Ready to actually do the setup? Jump straight to **[Day 1: Zero to Running Code](/onboarding/day-one-walkthrough)** — the executable checklist that takes you from no access to running CrestPet locally and shipping your first commit.
</Note>
