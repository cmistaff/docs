## What to Expect in Week 1

A rough timeline so you know what good progress looks like.

- **Day 1.** Accounts active, devbox reachable via Tailscale, video watched, Day 1 walkthrough complete.
- **Day 2.** CrestPetSystem running locally end to end. First Linear issue assigned for orientation.
- **Day 3.** First small PR opened against a sandboxed branch. Pair with your onboarding buddy on review.
- **Day 4.** Environment variables, AWS SSO, and the AI tooling stack configured. Comfortable in the PR review protocol.
- **Day 5.** First substantive PR merged. Sync with Michael on goals for Week 2.

## Where to Go Next

Once you have the basics running, branch out:

- The [Crest Handbook](/crest-corp/welcome) covers HR setup, policies, and the engineering rules every contributor follows.
- The [Crest Pet System](/architecture/overview) tab is your reference for the main product architecture, features, and infrastructure.
- The [docs homepage](https://docs.crestadvisorygroup.com/) lists every section of the docs site.

If anything in this guide is wrong, missing, or out of date, ping Michael on Slack or open a PR against `cmistaff/docs`. Onboarding docs decay fast and we rely on fresh eyes to keep them honest.
